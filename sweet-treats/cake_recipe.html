Cakes are an important part of every occasion such as birthdays, anniversaries or any other celebrations. Here is a simple and delicious recipe of a butter cake which can be prepared quickly and enjoyed with families and friends

Butter Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour - 150gm
  • Butter - 150gm
  • Sugar - 150gm
  • Egg - 3
  • Baking powder - 1 tsp
  • Vanila essence - 1 tsp

Time Taken

  • Prep Time: 15 min
  • Cook Time: 25 min

Method

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 180° celsius.
  2. Sift the flour and baking powder together and keep aside.
  3. Mix the butter and sugar with a cake mixer until the mixture is fluffy and smooth.
  4. Beat the eggs and add vanilla essence to it. Add the eggs to the butter and sugar mixture. Beat until all ingredients are mixed well.
  5. Pour the cake mixture into a baking pan and bake for 25 min at 180° temperature.
